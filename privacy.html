Privacy Policy

SEO Auditor Pro · Last updated: March 2026

Summary: SEO Auditor Pro does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data stays on your device.

📦 What Data We Access

SEO Auditor Pro reads the following data from the currently active browser tab only when you open the extension:

  • Page title, meta description, and meta tags
  • Heading tags (H1–H6) and their text content
  • Link URLs (internal and external)
  • Image elements and their alt attributes
  • Schema markup (JSON-LD scripts)
  • Open Graph and Twitter card tags
  • Hreflang tags
  • Page body text (for readability and keyword analysis)
  • HTTP response headers (for X-Robots-Tag check)

💾 What We Store Locally

The following data is stored only on your device using Chrome's local storage API (chrome.storage.local). It never leaves your browser:

  • Audit History — URLs and SEO scores of pages you've audited (last 20 entries)
  • Compare Page — SEO data of the page you saved for comparison
  • Theme Preference — Your light/dark mode setting
  • Score Weights — Your custom SEO score weightage settings

You can clear all stored data at any time from the History tab or Settings tab inside the extension.

🌐 External API Calls

SEO Auditor Pro makes the following outbound requests only when you explicitly trigger them:

  • Google PageSpeed Insights API — Only when you click Mobile/Desktop in the Speed tab. The URL of the current page is sent to Google's public API. Google's own Privacy Policy applies.
  • Broken Link Checker — When you click "Check for Broken Links", HEAD requests are sent to each link URL to check their status. No personal data is included.

No data is ever sent to any server operated by the developer of this extension.

🚫 What We Do NOT Do

  • We do NOT collect any personally identifiable information
  • We do NOT track your browsing history
  • We do NOT send any page data to our servers (we have none)
  • We do NOT show advertisements
  • We do NOT share any data with third parties
  • We do NOT use cookies or tracking pixels

🔐 Permissions Explained

  • activeTab — Read the current tab's URL and title
  • scripting — Inject the SEO scraper script into the current page to read DOM data
  • storage — Save your preferences and history locally on your device
  • host_permissions (<all_urls>) — Required to check link status (broken link checker) and fetch HTTP headers on any website
